About Us

Dinkapedia the language learning website

Dinkapedia's goal is to provide both information and resources for immersing into Dinka Language. Home to almost 2 million people, It gives and oppurtunity to learn to your hearts content. Understanding the Dinka tribe has many dialects, accents and clans, this platorm tries to diversify there content and does not claim to be a standard form of language.

Learning how to speak

The goals of the learning section are to properly translate english to dinka as accuretly as possible. It provides flashcards, lesson paths with different units, and auto learning. The end goal is to be able to communicate with dinka people, it will include slang, dialects, and words used in other languages.
